3 Ways to Memorise Concepts Immediately

Want to remember concepts but they are long and daunting? Here's a step-by-step guide to memorise them now.

1. Using Acronyms

Acronyms are made by taking the first letter from a few words and putting them together to form a phrase or sentence.For example, USA is a acronym. Taking the intial letter of United States (of) America, U-S-A is formed.
Acronyms make it easier for us to remember long words, and we can apply it to our studies as well. Instead of blindly repeating: Parentheses, Exponents, Multiplcation, Divison, Addition, Subraction (An order of calcultion) which does not have any pattern or attributes that makes it easier to remember, we can try to remember: PEMDAS.
